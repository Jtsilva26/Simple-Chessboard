Pawn:
Movement: one space going forward (towards enemey)
Attack: Horizontal attack if piece is there
Rook:
Movement: cross shape until its a wall or enemey
Attack: cross shape
Bishop:
Movement: Horizontal shape until its a wall or enemey
Attack: Horizontal attack
knight:
Movement: 3 forward & backwards one to the left or right (shape of an L)
Attack: At the edge of the L
Queen:
Movement: takes the Movement of rook and bishop
Attack: Horizontal & Cross
King:
Movement: one space going forward or backwards
Attack: Can attack unless it puts the king in check
