History of Chess

The Game of chess is a strategy game that involes two players and a total of sixteen pieces each. Every piece has it's own role and can play a huge part in whether the player is successful. On the front line you have your Pawns, there is a total of 8 pawns per player, 2 Rooks, 2 Knights, 2 Bishops, 1 Queen, and 1 king. Furthermore chess is one of the older strategy games stilled played today. The idea of chess is similiar to shogi and how it was used to develop war strategy and increase quick thinking for generals or leaders. Overall chess is a very fun game and still played competitively all over the world.
